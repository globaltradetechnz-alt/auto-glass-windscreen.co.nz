Windscreen Repair

At Auto Glass Windscreen, we offer professional windscreen chip and crack repair. Repairing a small chip is much cheaper than replacing the entire windscreen, and it can prevent the damage from spreading.

Our technicians use high-quality resin to fill chips and cracks, restoring the structural integrity of your windscreen and improving visibility. Most repairs can be completed in under 30 minutes at your location.

When to Repair vs Replace

  • ✓ Chips smaller than a $2 coin — usually repairable
  • ✓ Cracks up to 75mm — often repairable
  • ✓ Damage in the driver's line of sight — may need replacement
  • ✓ Multiple cracks or large damage — replacement recommended

Repair Process

  • ✓ Clean the damaged area
  • ✓ Inject high-quality resin into the chip or crack
  • ✓ Cure the resin with UV light
  • ✓ Polish for a clear finish
